# Accounting Reports

Accounting reports are structured financial summaries generated from recorded transactions. They help businesses understand their financial performance, financial position, and overall financial health.

In Simple Accounting, report are automatically generated from [imported journals](/journals/import). Once transactions are properly recorded and balanced, the system processes them into standardized Accounting Reports:

- [General Ledger](/accounting-reports/general-ledger)
- [Subledger](/accounting-reports/subledger)
- [Trial Balance](/accounting-reports/trial-balance)
- [Profit & Loss](/accounting-reports/profit-and-loss)
- [Balance Sheet](/accounting-reports/balance-sheet)
