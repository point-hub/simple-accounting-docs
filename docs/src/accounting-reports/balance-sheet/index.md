# Balance Sheet

A Balance Sheet is a financial report that shows the financial position of a business at a specific point in time.

It summarizes what the business:

- Owns (Assets)
- Owes (Liabilities)
- Invested by the owner (Equity)

:::info
In Simple Accounting, the General Ledger is generated automatically from [imported journals](/journals/import).

- Asset accounts are grouped under coa_type = assets
- Liability accounts are grouped under coa_type = liability
- Equity accounts are grouped under coa_type = equity
:::

::: info FORMULA
The Balance Sheet is based on the fundamental accounting equation

**Assets** = **Liabilities** + **Equity**
:::
